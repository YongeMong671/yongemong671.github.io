Why WebPageTest?

WebPageTest is the gold standard tool for enhancing website speed, user experience, conversion rates, and SEO rankings via Core Web Vitals. It offers deep diagnostics and actionable insights for measuring and optimizing performance across all major browsers, device types, and network conditions.

Originally created and open-sourced by Patrick Meenan in 2008, WebPageTest was acquired by Catchpoint in 2020 and incorporated into Catchpoints industry-leading Internet Performance Monitoring (IPM) Platform. This update has strengthened WebPageTest, making it a part of Catchpoints comprehensive suite of AI-powered enterprise solutions, including Real User Monitoring (RUM), Session Replay, SSO, Security, SLO monitoring powered by 3000+ intelligent agents worldwide.

While Catchpoint continues to grow stronger every day, its mission remains unchanged: empowering you to deliver the best possible experiences to your users, no matter where they are in the world.

WebPageTest Open Source. Its code remains freely accessible under the Polyform Shield license, continuing its legacy of openness and community-driven innovation.
